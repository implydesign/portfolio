Locatour — Travelling Mobile App

Locatour is a mobile application providing personalized travel recommendations through user-generated content.

App Information

Background

Locatour turns social media into a personalized search engine for travelers.

Recommendation System

The app personalizes recommendations through content-based and collaborative filtering.

User Needs

Locatour connects travelers and helps them find services and communities in new places.

Safety

Users are verified, and community guidelines ensure a secure environment.

Community

Users create groups based on places or interests, fostering local engagement.
